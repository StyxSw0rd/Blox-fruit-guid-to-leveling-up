How to get a fruit

There is 2 ways of getting a fruit.One way is to roll a fruit from the blox fruit gacha or to buy it from the blox fruit seller

Fruit types

There is 3 types of fruits.

  1. Elemental: a fruit that gives you immunty from fighting syles, gun and sword (unless you use haki on it)
  2. Beast: a fruit that transforms a player into a beast
  3. Natural: a fruit that gives you buffs like speed and damage

Rarities

Common: Cheapest Fruits and more likely to spawn
Uncommon: Cheap Fruits
Rare: Mid-priced Fruits
Legendary: Expensive Fruit
Mythical: Very Expensive Fruits and have the least likely chance of spawning

Fruit Awakening

You can awaken 11 fruits in total.
To awaken a fruit you need to do a raid to earn fragments.
After getting enough fragments you can do a raid of the fruit
(e.g if u have flame fruit you could do a flame raid)
and finish it teleports you to a npc called Mysterious Entity
Once you awaken the fruit it will teleport where you set you home point.

Trading/Storing

When you get a physical fruit, you can click on it and press to store it in your inventory.
You can trade the fruit you stored by going to the mansion or the cafe in third sea and second sea.
You can also trade gamepass such as(recomended):perm budha, x2 beli and +1 storage.
